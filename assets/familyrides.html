FAMILY RIDES

Wonder Splash

The Wonder Splash is a fun-filled boat ride that concludes with a dramatic splashdown. Ideal for families, this ride offers a gentle yet exciting journey through water before ending with a splash that soaks both riders and onlookers. It’s a perfect way for families to enjoy a shared adventure and cool off together.

Pirate Ship

The Pirate Ship ride is a classic family attraction designed to mimic the motion of a swinging pirate ship. This pendulum ride gently rocks back and forth, offering a mild thrill suitable for all ages. It’s a great choice for families looking for a fun and moderate ride experience.

Magic Mushroom

The Magic Mushroom is an indoor play area specifically designed for younger children. Featuring interactive elements and playful activities, this area provides a safe and engaging environment where kids can explore and enjoy various attractions tailored to their age group.

Caterpillar Coaster

The Caterpillar Coaster is a mild roller coaster designed for children and families. With gentle twists and turns, this coaster offers a fun introduction to roller coasters without the extreme speeds and inversions of more intense rides. It’s a great way for younger guests to experience the thrill of a roller coaster in a family-friendly setting.

Carousel

The Carousel is a classic merry-go-round featuring beautifully decorated horses and vibrant music. This timeless attraction provides a delightful and nostalgic ride experience, perfect for families and guests of all ages looking to enjoy a traditional amusement ride.
