KIDDIE RIDES

Mini Express

The Mini Express is a charming train ride that takes young children on a scenic tour around the park. Designed with small passengers in mind, this gentle ride offers a delightful experience for kids as they enjoy the sights and sounds of the park from the comfort of a miniature train.

Flying Jumbo

The Flying Jumbo is an elephant-themed ride that gently lifts and spins kids around in the air. Designed for younger guests, this ride provides a whimsical and fun experience as children soar through the air on colorful, flying elephants.

Mini Pirate Ship

The Mini Pirate Ship is a scaled-down version of the classic pirate ship ride, designed specifically for younger children. With a gentle swinging motion, it offers a fun and family-friendly experience that captures the spirit of adventure without the intensity of larger rides.

Kiddies Wheel

The Kiddies Wheel is a small Ferris wheel designed just for kids. With low, gentle rotations, this ride offers a safe and enjoyable way for children to experience the view from above, providing a sense of excitement and wonder in a kid-friendly setting.
