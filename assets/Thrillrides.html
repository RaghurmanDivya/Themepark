THRILL RIDES

Recoil

Experience the thrill of the Recoil, a reverse looping roller coaster that offers an intense and exhilarating ride. As you soar through high speeds and unexpected twists, the coaster takes you through multiple inversions and thrilling drops. Its unique design ensures a reverse journey through the track, making it a must-try for adrenaline junkies seeking an unforgettable ride.

Maverick

Experience the Maverick, a ride with unpredictable spins and flips, offering intense thrill. This ride combines rapid accelerations with sudden changes in direction to deliver an exhilarating experience. Perfect for those who crave excitement, Maverick promises a roller coaster experience unlike any other.

Flash Tower

The Flash Tower offers a heart-pounding free-fall experience from a towering height. As you ascend, anticipation builds before you plunge down at high speed, giving you a rush of adrenaline and a sensation of weightlessness. This ride is ideal for those who love the thrill of heights and rapid descents.

Y-Scream

The Y-Scream is a thrilling pendulum ride that swings you high into the air while rotating in a circular motion. The combination of height and rotation provides a unique sensation of both weightlessness and g-forces. It's a thrilling choice for those who enjoy the sensation of flying and spinning.
