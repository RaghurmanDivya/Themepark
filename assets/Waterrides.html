WATER RIDES

Rain Disco

The Rain Disco is a vibrant and immersive water ride where you can dance under synchronized rain showers while enjoying lively music and colorful lights. This ride combines the fun of a dance party with the refreshing experience of getting wet, making it a favorite for those looking to have fun and cool off simultaneously.

Wave Pools

Wave Pools offer a beach-like experience with artificial waves that mimic the ocean. Perfect for those who enjoy the feeling of being at the shore, these pools provide a safe and fun environment to swim and play in waves of various sizes. It's an ideal spot for both relaxation and playful water activities.

Lazy River

The Lazy River is a relaxing water ride that takes you along a meandering channel at a gentle pace. As you float on an inner tube, you can enjoy a serene and leisurely experience, making it perfect for unwinding and enjoying the scenic surroundings of the water park.

Boomerang

The Boomerang is a high-thrill water slide that offers an exciting ride through a giant funnel. You’ll zoom down the slide before being propelled up the other side, experiencing a mix of high speeds and moments of weightlessness. It’s a thrilling water attraction that guarantees a splashy and memorable adventure.

Vertical Drop

For those who love a rush of adrenaline, the Vertical Drop provides a near-vertical water slide that delivers a free-fall sensation. This ride offers a high-speed plunge that will leave you breathless as you experience the thrill of descending rapidly through a water-filled chute.
