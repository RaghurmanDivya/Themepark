OTHER ATTRACTIONS

4D Theater

The 4D Theater provides an immersive cinematic experience with 3D visuals, motion seats, and special effects like wind, water, and scent. This attraction takes movie-watching to the next level by engaging multiple senses, making you feel as though you are part of the action.

Musical Fountain & Laser Show

The Musical Fountain & Laser Show is a spectacular evening attraction featuring a synchronized display of lights, water, and music. This vibrant show combines colorful lasers with dynamic water effects, creating a mesmerizing visual and auditory experience that captivates audiences of all ages.

Virtual Reality Experience

The Virtual Reality Experience offers cutting-edge VR games and simulations that transport you to different worlds. Using advanced VR technology, guests can immerse themselves in a variety of virtual environments, from thrilling adventures to imaginative scenarios, making it a unique and exciting attraction.
